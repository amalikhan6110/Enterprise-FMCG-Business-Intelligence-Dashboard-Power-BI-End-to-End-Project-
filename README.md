# 🏭 FMCG Retail Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution for the FMCG retail industry using Power BI. It focuses on analyzing sales performance, revenue trends, product demand, and channel effectiveness.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze sales and revenue performance across regions and time
- Evaluate promotion and discount impact on revenue
- Identify high-performing products and categories
- Provide actionable insights for business decision-making

---

## 📊 Dashboards Included

### 1️⃣ Sales Analysis Dashboard
- Total Revenue, Gross Profit, Units Sold
- Revenue trends by month and weekday
- Sales by city and store location
- Product category performance
- Channel-wise revenue distribution

### 2️⃣ Revenue Analysis Dashboard
- Net Revenue, Discounted Revenue, Growth %
- Revenue by products and cities
- High discounted & high demand products
- Discount impact analysis
- Channel performance comparison

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
- Promotions increase sales volume but reduce profit margins
- Certain cities dominate revenue contribution
- High discounts do not always guarantee higher revenue
- Product demand varies significantly across categories

---

## 🏗️ Data Model
- Star Schema (Fact + Dimensions)
- Fact Table: Sales Data
- Dimensions: Date, Product, Store, Supplier

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query (ETL)
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling (Star Schema)

---

## 🚀 Business Impact
This dashboard helps stakeholders:
- Monitor performance in real-time
- Optimize pricing and promotions
- Improve product and inventory strategy
- Make data-driven decisions
